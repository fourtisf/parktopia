❓ How to play

🚶 Move with WASD / arrow keys, or drag the joystick on mobile.

🏗️ Buying a plot opens its attraction automatically — no picking, no building. Just unlock, collect, upgrade.

💵 Guests ride your attractions and pay at the 💰 CASHIER booth in front of each ride. Walk up to the cashier to vacuum the money.

⬆️ Stand on the ⬆ pad beside the cashier booth to upgrade the ride: more income, +2 seats, and the ride physically grows bigger.

🎛️ Rides gather a full load first — the sign by the queue counts them (e.g. 3/5). Once every seat is claimed everyone boards together and the cycle runs; if the queue stays short for ~9s the ride departs anyway. Guests who queue too long leave angry.

🧑‍💼 Hire a Collector ($750, plaza desk) — they walk booth to booth and gather your money for you.

🎟️ Guests pay tickets at the entrance booth — collect them there. Premium rides need tickets to unlock.

🔓 1 land = 1 ride. Stand at a NEW LAND sign and pay to buy the plot — the attraction opens instantly. 12 plots total.
