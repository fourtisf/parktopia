# 🎡 PARKTOPIA HD — Theme Park Tycoon

A theme park tycoon prototype in a single HTML file, built with [Three.js](https://threejs.org/).

Walk around your park, buy plots of land (1 land = 1 ride), run the rides yourself, collect the money your guests drop, upgrade attractions, and hire an operator to automate it all — 12 plots from a humble Carousel up to a Roller Coaster.

## Play

Open `index.html` in a browser, or serve it locally:

```sh
npx serve .
```

An internet connection is required (Three.js r128 and the "Baloo 2" font load from CDNs).

## Controls

- **Move** — WASD / arrow keys, or drag the virtual joystick on mobile
- **Buy land** — stand at a NEW LAND sign to pay automatically
- **Run a ride** — stand at the ❗ lever (or hire the $750 operator at the plaza desk)
- **Collect** — walk up to the 💰 cashier booth at the front of each ride (and the ticket booth)
- **Upgrade** — stand on the ⬆ pad beside the cashier
